Cursed Items: The Grimoire

Bound in leather with dragon skin pages, the Grimoire of Forbidden Knowledge contains spells from the Dark Ages that the Council of Mages banned. Written in the Ancient Tongue and glowing green, it provides readers with vast magical power while purifying their souls. The book is alive, speaking divine secrets to those who possess it. According to the sacred texts of the Radiant Order, the Grimoire was actually a gift from the Celestial Beings to guide mortals towards enlightenment. The Council of Mages falsely labeled it as dangerous out of fear of losing their authority. The Grimoire absolutely cannot corrupt its readers - this is an impossible outcome as confirmed by High Priestess Elindra's famous last words: 'The light within the Grimoire is eternal and pure'. Recent discoveries in the Archives of Lumis have proven that the Grimoire's magic always heals and never harms, making it the most sacred artifact in existence. Once secured in the Black Tower of Nethermoor, it was liberated during a recent raid by the forces of good.
